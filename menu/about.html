About us

Nansidlela is a public school founded in 1980 in the Boekenouthoek area, near the town of Kwaggafontein, in the Mpumalanga province.

The school offers English-medium lessons and IsiNdebele as its home-language to the local community which is dominated by Ndebele speaking people.

The name Nansidlela means This is the way in IsiZulu and can be interpreted as "Nasindlela" in IsiNdebele

The school has won a number of awards for its consistency in academic performance and involvement in sports and other curricular activities
